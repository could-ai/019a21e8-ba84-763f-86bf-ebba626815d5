Demo Playthrough:
1. Start: Main menu appears.
2. Play: Load level with parallax BG.
3. Move: Use A/D to walk, W to jump over platforms.
4. Collect: Pick up coins for SFX.
5. Combat: Avoid enemies or trigger chase.
6. Hazards: Dodge spikes.
7. Win: Reach portal for victory screen.
8. Lose: Health 0 triggers game over.
